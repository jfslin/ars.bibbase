The Automated Rehabilitation System (ARS) is a rehabilitation exercise motion supervision system designed to supplement physiotherapists (PTs) with real-time measurement data and automated charting, allowing PTs to focus on patient safety and diagnosis. ARS utilizes multiple light-weight body-worn inertial measurement units (IMUs) to perform:
  • goniometry, the estimation of the joint angles of limbs of interest,
  • motion segmentation, the estimation of the starting and ending points of exercise repetitions,
  • provide real-time visual feedback to patients on exercise performance, and
  • automatically document the collected data for PT review for changing patient performance over rehabilitation visits and inspect the data for exercise inconsistencies such as joint compensation.
